Custom Handcrafted Leather Products

At Simpson Leather Craft, all physical leather crafted items are hand made. Tooling is completely custom. Holes are hand punched. The stitching is all saddle stitched by hand. This has allowed more attention to be placed on the final products. Laser engraving, for more detailed designs, has recently been introduced as our only "machine" used.
